About Moon

The artist Moon, is a part singer-songwriter, a part Physiotherapist and Software developer, with his first first release scheduled for 12/12/2023. His work is inspired by some of the greatest hits from 60's blues to 80's funk, to country, indie, rock, and classical Opera. As a growing artist in the 21st century, he is sure to bring some of the greatest hits yet.
